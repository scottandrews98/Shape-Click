How To Play

The aim of the game is for you to click on all the circles on the screen as quick as possible. Your score will be the time in seconds in which it took for you to complete the level. Your score will then be recorded on the level select screen below the level number. Once you've clicked on all the circles on the screen then the game will display your score then take you back to the level select screen. Once all the levels are completed then you can go back and try to improve on your score.

Good Luck!
